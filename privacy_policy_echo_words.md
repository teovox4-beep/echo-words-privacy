## 1. Introduction

Echo Words ("the App") is developed by Teo Studios. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

By using Echo Words, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Automatically Collected Information

- **Anonymous User ID**: We use Firebase Anonymous Authentication to assign each user a unique anonymous identifier. No personal information (name, email, phone) is required or collected.
- **Game Progress**: Your scores, badges, streaks, and game progress are stored locally on your device and optionally synced to Firebase Firestore.
- **Device Information**: Basic device information may be collected by Firebase Analytics (device type, OS version, app version).

### 2.2 Information You Do NOT Need to Provide

- No account registration required
- No email address required
- No real name required
- No location data collected

---

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

- Save and sync your game progress across sessions
- Provide daily challenges and streak tracking
- Improve app performance and fix bugs
- Send app updates via Firebase Remote Config

## 5. Advertisements

Echo Words displays **optional rewarded advertisements** through Google AdMob.

- Ads are **voluntary** — you choose to watch them to earn in-game coins
- We limit rewarded ads to **5 per day**
- AdMob may collect device identifiers for ad personalization
- You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings

---

## 6. Data Storage and Security

- Game data is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage
- Cloud sync is performed via Firebase Firestore (encrypted in transit)
- We do not sell your data to third parties
- Anonymous user IDs cannot be linked to your real identity

---

## 7. Children's Privacy

Echo Words is rated for users **13 years and older**. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us at teovox4@gmail.com.

---

## 8. Data Retention

- Local data remains on your device until you reset progress or uninstall the app
- Firebase data is retained for as long as your anonymous account is active
- You can request deletion of your data at any time (see Section 10)

---

## 9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access**: Request information about data we hold
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your data (see Section 10)
- **Opt-out**: Disable analytics in device settings

To exercise these rights, contact: **teovox4@gmail.com**

---

## 10. Account Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time:

- **In-app:** Open the app → Settings → **Delete Account & Data** → Confirm
- **Web-based:** Visit our [Account Deletion page](https://teovox4-beep.github.io/echo-words-privacy/delete-account)
- **By email:** Send a request to teovox4@gmail.com with subject "Delete Echo Words Account"

Account deletion is **permanent and irreversible**. All cloud-synced data and your anonymous user account will be removed.
